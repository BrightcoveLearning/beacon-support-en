Apple Store

Platform: iOS / tvOS

At the moment, there is no option for scheduling the App availability in the App Store. Once Apple has approved the App, it will be available on the App Store.

Google Play Store

Platform: Android / Android TV

At the moment, is not possible to schedule when the App will go Live in the Play Store, but is possible to schedule when the following updates to the App will be released/published.
  • Submit your iOS / tvOS App for review
  • App Submission statuses
  
  • Publish your Android app
  • Control when app changes are published

Samsung

Platform: Samsung Smart TV

At the moment, there is no option for scheduling the App availability in the Samsung Apps TV. After passing the test and completing the application registration, if there are no defects, your application is published on Samsung Apps TV.

Amazon IAP

Platform: Amazon Fire TV

Amazon has recently added the ability to specify the date and time you would like your app to go live on the Amazon Appstore. This gives you the ability to coordinate your app release on Amazon with releases on other stores and in conjunction with any press or social media launch events you may wish to plan.
  • Application Publication Process
  
  • Launching Apps on Your Schedule in the Amazon Appstore

Roku

Platform: Roku

It is possible to schedule the App/Channel release in the Schedule Publishing option in the Developer Dashboard > Add Channel section.

Note: You will not be able to choose a date or time that is earlier than the second business day from the current moment, nor will you be able to schedule publication for a Friday or a weekend.
  • Roku: Schedule Publishing
