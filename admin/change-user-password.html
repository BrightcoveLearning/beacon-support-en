Introduction

At times you may need to change passwords for users because they have forgotten it or it has been compromised. To this, follow the steps below.

Note: you must be an administrator on the account to perform these steps.

Steps

  1. Login to Beacon.
  2. Click your name in the upper-right corner and select Users from the dropdown:
  3. Either page through or search the users to find the one you want:
  4. Click on the user's name to open the details.
  5. Enter a new password in the New Password field, and then type it again in the Confirm Password field:
  6. Click Update User, and the password will be updated.
