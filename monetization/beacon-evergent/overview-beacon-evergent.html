Introduction

Evergent is a payment and subscriber management system that has been integrated with Beacon to provide these capabilities. Evergent provides robust subscriber management and access to many popular payment gateways worldwide, including those used commonly in Latin America, the Middle East, Africa, and the Asia-Pacific region.

Glossary of terms

Business Unit
A Business Unit (BU) is an entity in Evergent’s system that defines a country or region where you would like to sell your content. You will need separate Business Units if you want different currencies or tax collection per country/region. In Evergent’s system each Business Unit will have a currency assigned to it, this currency is what will be presented to their users when they checkout.
Payment Gateway
A service that allows a merchant to accept payments from customers. The payment gateway acts as a middleman by moving the money from the bank accounts of your customers to your own bank account in a secure and reliable way.
Merchant of Record
The legal entity that is authorized to receive payments from consumers. You be the MOR (Merchant of Record) with each gateway that you use.
Merchant Account
A virtual bank account where revenue from online sales is sent. Revenue resides in your merchant account until settlement — the process of transferring funds to your bank account. Typically the payment gateway provides a merchant account. Gateways that do not provide merchant accounts transfer funds directly to your bank account.
Package
A Package defines how users can purchase access to your content in Beacon CMS, it defines all of the details for a monetization plan. It includes information such as the price, billing frequency, availability, app stores availability, currency, trial period, etc.
Products
Products in Evergent defines all of the details for a monetization plan. It includes information such as the price, billing frequency, availability, app stores availability, currency, trial period, etc.

Account setup

What's needed:

Beacon

  • In Beacon, you need a CMS with Evergent configured as a Store.
  • All of the countries where the customers’ service will be available.
  • Each country should have a currency defined for receiving payments from a viewer.

Evergent

  • You need Business Units created in Evergent that map to the currencies that you want to receive payments in.
  • Each Business Unit will have gateways and app store billing (Apple Store, Roku, Amazon, Google Play) configured. You are required to have a relationship and merchant account with each gateway they want to use.

Package/Product management

You will set up and manage monetization plans from both the Beacon UI and Evergent UI. Plans will need to be manually linked using SKUs in both Beacon and Evergent:

  • The Packages that are configured in Beacon are how information gets presented to the viewers in the app. See Creating a Subscription Package for details.
  • The Product in Evergent allows them to process payments for the correct monetization plan and validate the receipt before the viewer is granted access to the content. See Evergent Account Management for details.
  • For the initial release, Beacon will support the following monetization models:
    • MixedVOD - subscription for Authenticated users with Ads served
    • SVOD - subscription for Authenticated users and no Ads served
  • Future releases will include TVOD and PPV

Website sales

Once Packages and Products are configured, a customer will be able to accept payments on their Beacon Web app. Purchases in the Beacon web app are processed through a payment gateway connected through the Evergent platform.

Payment Gateways and Payment methods

Evergent allows our customers to receive payments through multiple payment gateways. The payment gateway configuration is at the Business Unit level. A customer can have multiple payment gateways configured to a Business Unit. When multiple gateways are configured, the viewer has different payment method options when checking out. Customers are required to have a relationship directly with the payment gateway.

In app purchases

Beacon customers will also be able to sell their content through Apple, Google, Amazon and Roku stores. Each store is configured as an option in the Beacon CMS and in Evergent’s system.

  • When creating a SVOD Package in Beacon, customers have the option to select which app stores they want to sell their Package through. The can set the app store, availability , location, price, currency and app store product sku.
  • In addition, the app store (Apple, Google, Amazon, Roku), app store Product ID, and Product Name can be configured on a SVOD Product.

Subscriber/viewer management

The Beacon-Evergent integration supports registration and purchase workflows through both apps:

  • When viewers register in the Beacon app, a shadow viewer is automatically created in Evergent’s viewer database.
  • Viewers are segmented in Evergent’s viewer database. If a customer has more than one Business Unit, viewers are created in the Business Unit that corresponds to their location.
  • Viewers can either be non-paying viewers or paying viewers in Evergent’s system.

Troubleshooting viewer issues

You will need to use both Beacon’s Viewer Management and Evergent’s Subscriber management to troubleshoot your viewers' issues, depending on the kind of issue. The information below will help guide you as to which system you will need to go to for particular cases.

Viewer information contained in Beacon Viewer Management:

  • name
  • email
  • password
  • purchase history
  • profile information
  • bookmarks and favorites
  • devices associated with the account

Viewer information contained in Evergent:

  • name
  • email
  • payment credentials
  • purchase history

Tasks that must be completed in Beacon

  • Update Viewer password
  • Edit or delete profile
  • Remove devices from Beacon account

Tasks that must be completed in Evergent

  • Issue a refund to a customers payment method on file
  • Cancel a viewer's subscription
  • Verify that a viewer payment was successful
  • Check the renewal date of a viewer's subscription
  • See all of a viewer's payments

Coupons and voucher codes

In Evergent UI, you will be able to generate coupons and vouchers for discounts. Once you generate these codes, you can distribute them to your viewers, and then viewers can redeem them during the checkout process. Coupons and vouchers are associated with specific SVOD Packages/Products, so viewers can only redeem them with that corresponding SVOD Package/Products.

Viewer communication

You can configure the content and style of the emails you send to viewers in Evergent’s system. You can also configure which events trigger emails being sent. For example, when a viewer signs up for the service or when they upgrade from one subscription to another.

Analytics and reporting

Financial reports

Business performance reporting will be available in Evergent’s reporting Dashboard to help you understand how your Beacon app is performing in relation to your business objectives.
