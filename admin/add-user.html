Introduction

When you want to add a new user to your Beacon account, follow the steps below.

Note: you must be an administrator on the account to perform these steps.

Steps

  1. Login to Beacon.
  2. Click your name in the upper-right corner and select Users from the dropdown:
  3. Click Add New User:
  4. In the dialog that opens, enter the user's full name and email address, a password, and select a role for the user:
  5. Enter a new password in the New Password field, and then type it again in the Confirm Password field:
  6. Click Register, and the new user will will be notified by email that they have been added to the account..
